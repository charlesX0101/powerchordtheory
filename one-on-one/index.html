One-on-One

PowerChord Theory is built on the idea that most people can learn on their own. With the amount of information available today, self-teaching is a natural path for many musicians. The challenge is often knowing what to focus on and how to move forward with clarity.

One-on-one sessions exist for players who benefit from additional direction and structured support.

Session Focus

One-on-one sessions are centered on groove, time, touch, and musical awareness. Technique and theory are introduced only as they become useful. Sessions adapt to the individual and are shaped by current ability and goals.

Custom Materials

Students receive practice materials tailored to their level and needs. These include professionally developed backing tracks, exercises, and song-based studies created specifically for each player. Materials are produced in a recording environment and designed to support real musical growth.

Groove-Based Approach

The learning model prioritizes rhythm, feel, and consistency. Songs are used as tools to explore musical ideas and develop listening skills. The approach reflects how musicians develop in real playing situations.

Coaching Experience

One-on-one guidance is informed by professional experience in performance, recording, touring, and formal study. The focus remains on practical skills that translate beyond the practice space.

Gear Support

Guidance can include instrument selection, practice setups, amplification, and recording basics. Gear advice is practical and goal-driven, with dedicated sessions available for buying support.

Format

Sessions are offered remotely by default, with hybrid options available for local players. Availability is intentionally limited to maintain quality and focus.
