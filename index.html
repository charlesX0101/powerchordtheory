A Practical Approach to Learning Bass and Guitar, Focused on Rhythm and Feel.

PowerChordTheory is set up to help people get moving without needing to sort through everything on their own first. The focus is on making the early stages of learning feel clearer, whether someone is just starting or coming back to an instrument after some time away.

Everything here is organized to help people get started, make sense of what they’re learning, and keep moving without getting overwhelmed. The navigation below can be used to explore practical guidance, core ideas, and optional support as it’s useful.

Core Sections

Gear Guide

Clear guidance on choosing instruments and essential gear. Focused on what matters early and what can wait.

Learn

Core concepts that support rhythm, harmony, and playing real music. Built around curated videos and simple explanations.

One-On-One

Personal, one on one direct support for learning and gear decisions. Available for those who want extra guidance without pressure.
