Power Chord Theory

Many musicians start by teaching themselves how to play. They pick things up by listening, copying, experimenting, and slowly putting pieces together. Today, that process is more accessible than ever. Between YouTube, online reference sites, recordings, and forums, almost everything needed to learn is available for free.

Now that finding information is no longer an issue, what matters is knowing what to do with it, what to focus on first, and how to keep moving when things start to feel unclear.

Power Chord Theory brings together the kind of information self-taught players already rely on and organizes it around the early stages of actually playing music. The focus is on turning interest into action by addressing the questions that tend to stall new and returning players from getting an instrument in their hands to playing real songs and maybe even their first gig.
